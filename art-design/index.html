About Portraits

Process

From photographs of your pet, Jeannette creates an expressive and lovingly rendered portrait. After the creative process, the portrait is delivered, ready for display.

About Me

Medium

Jeannette creates portraits using oil paints. She uses artist-quality archival materials, so you will enjoy the portrait for a lifetime. Check out some of the portraits in the gallery

View Gallery

Dimensions

If you have a specific size requirement, Jeannette can customize canvas sizes. The following standard dimensions are available.

  • 9 x 12" ($170)
  • 11 x 14" ($250)
  • 16 x 20" ($500)

Display

Paintings are delivered unframed, wired, and are ready to hang and admire. Check out the gallery for display examples.

About Jeannette

Jeannette is a "Jill of all trades". She is a trained artist, gardener, culture enthusiast, dog-lover, and computer programmer living in Victoria BC. She graduated from University of Victoria in 2010 with a Bachelor's Degree in Fine Arts. She has created art for commercial galleries, and painted many dogs for happy clients! Currently, she is studying to master a new palette of technology in the Information and Computer Systems associate degree at Camosun College.
